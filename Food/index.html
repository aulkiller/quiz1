Bogor Bogar Food

These are my favorite snacks, trust me you would love them

Lapis Talas Bogor

A two layered cake or what we call lapis with talas special flavour with cheese topping that would make you won't stop munching on it.

Asinan Bogor

Fresh Veggie and Tropical Fruits mixed with vinegar and special peanut sauce combined with usual Asinan taste(salty) results in fresh yet healthy snacks.

Roti Unyil Bogor

The legendary and only one smoll bread from here which is satiating yet looks cute, with various flavour from strawberries to matcha.

Bir Kotjok

This is not just your daily drink. This one is actually healthy yet addicting due to mixture of red ginger, clove, sugar palm, and cinnamon.
