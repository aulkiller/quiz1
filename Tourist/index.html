Where to go???

Don't worry i have some recommendation for you

Kebon Raya Bogor

One of our premium attractions in the city. This garden features beautiful scenery and manicured lawns, you can aslo enjoy the refreshing breeze while playing around in the Maze or just laying around near the trees. You can also stroll in various international flora in Japanese Zen Garden and French Garden.

Check it out right here

Curug Bidadari Cikoneng

Also known as Nymph Waterfall. Not only having a beautiful scenery because its clear waters at the base but this waterfall also features a large pool that forms at the base and the edges of the pool are surrounded by a small sandy beach which is perfect for family vacation.

Check it out right here

Gunung Batu

This beautiful mountain which have 875 mdpl features another level of beauty. With smaller height compared to another mountain, even several person called it a hill. Due to its height this mountain could be climbed by almost everyone. You can also camps out if you like.

Check it out right here

Taman Safari Bogor

Another one of premium attractions in the city.Located at Puncak, This safari garden is built on a huge tea plantation which spanned 168 hectares of land. This safari park features both day and night safaris, you could also spot a huge number of animals in a semi-wild environtment.

Check it out right here

Ah Poong Sentul

One kind of Floating Market. You could find alot Bogor specialities in here while sightseeing or riding a boat. This floating market provides a unique dining experience while featuring not only food court but also some attraction near the river.

Check it out right here
